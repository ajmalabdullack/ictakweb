ICT Academy of Kerala is forming a Premium membership programme with selected Engineering/ Science/ Management institutions in the state. The institutions will be selected based on a set of criteria, through an online application form from the interested colleges. The submission will be scrutinized by the ICTAK administration office and if required, will interact with the applying institutions for obtaining clarifications. Selected institutions will be provided with one year membership and will be provided with one year membership and will be supported by the ICTAK on a set of activities that will help the students, teachers and institutions at large.
